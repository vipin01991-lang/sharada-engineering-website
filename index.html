Workshop Photo

Your Local Lathe & Machining Specialist

Sharada Engineering Works is a specialised lathe and machining workshop based in Iritty, Kannur, Kerala. We work on individual vehicle components – we are not a general automobile service centre and we do not repair full vehicles.

Local mechanics, garages, and workshops bring their parts to us for precision machining and reconditioning. From axles and drive shafts to gearbox parts and clutch components, we handle each job carefully to give your parts a second life.

✔ Trusted by Local Mechanics & Workshops

What We Do

Our Services

ഞങ്ങളുടെ സേവനങ്ങൾ

We specialise only in lathe and machining works. We do not provide full vehicle repair services.

Lathe Machining Works

Precision lathe operations for metal components. We handle turning, threading, and boring to exact measurements for vehicle and general engineering parts.

  • Metal Turning
  • Thread Cutting
  • Boring & Facing
  • Taper Turning
🔩

Vehicle Parts Reconditioning

Reconditioning of worn vehicle components to bring them back to correct size and working condition. We work on parts brought in by mechanics and garages.

  • Axle & Drive Shaft Reconditioning
  • Gearbox Parts Machining
  • Clutch Parts Finishing
  • Brake Drum Turning
🔧

Shaft & Axle Repair

Resizing, straightening, and finishing of shafts and axles that have worn beyond tolerance. Restored to original dimensions with proper surface finish.

  • Shaft Resizing & Turning
  • Axle Repair
  • Keyway Cutting
  • Surface Finishing
🛞

Bush Making & Fitting

Custom bushes machined to the exact size needed for your application. Material selected based on load and fit requirements. Pressed in using hydraulic press.

  • Custom Bush Machining
  • Bush Replacement & Fitting
  • Bearing Seat Preparation
  • Hydraulic Press Fitting
💧

Bearing Fitting

Proper bearing installation using hydraulic press to avoid damage during fitting. Shaft and housing surfaces prepared on lathe for correct interference fit.

  • Bearing Press Fitting
  • Shaft Preparation for Bearings
  • Housing Boring for Bearings
  • Bearing Removal
🔥

Basic Welding (Support Work)

Simple welding as support for machining jobs – for example, building up worn surfaces before machining, or minor crack repair on components before reconditioning.

  • Weld Build-Up for Reconditioning
  • Minor Crack Repair
  • Support Welding for Machined Parts

⚠️ We specialise only in lathe and machining works. We do not provide full vehicle repair or fabrication services.

Our Equipment

Workshop Machines

Our workshop is equipped with the right machines to handle precision lathe and machining jobs.

🔩

Lathe Machine

Our main machine. Used for turning, boring, threading, and facing metal components. Handles shafts, axles, bushes, and other vehicle parts to exact dimensions.

🔄

Drilling Machine

Pillar drilling machine for accurate hole-making in metal components. Used for drilling, reaming, and counterboring as needed during machining jobs.

🌀

Grinding Machine

Used for finishing and sizing metal parts to a smooth, precise surface. Ideal for final passes on shafts and other components requiring close tolerances.

💪

Hydraulic Press

Used for pressing bearings, bushes, and other press-fit components safely. Prevents damage during assembly and disassembly of shaft and housing assemblies.

⚡

Welding Machine (Support Use)

Used only as support for machining jobs – weld build-up on worn surfaces before turning, or minor repairs on components before reconditioning. Not a standalone welding service.
