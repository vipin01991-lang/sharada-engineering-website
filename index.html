About Us / ഞങ്ങളെക്കുറിച്ച്

Trusted Lathe Works in Iritty Since 1998

Sharada Engineering Works is Iritty's trusted lathe and vehicle component reconditioning specialist. Located in Kannur, Kerala, we serve the automotive and industrial sectors with precision and reliability.

With over 25 years of expertise, we specialize in shaft repair, axle reconditioning, bush and bearing fitting, water pump rebuilding, and precision lathe operations for vehicles and machinery.
