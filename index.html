About Us

Workshop Photo

Decades of Engineering Excellence

Sharada Engineering Works is a trusted mechanical workshop based in Iritty, Kannur, Kerala. With years of hands-on experience, we have built a reputation for delivering high-quality engineering solutions to individuals, farmers, transport operators, and businesses across northern Kerala.

Our workshop is equipped with modern machines operated by skilled technicians who take pride in precision and reliability. Whether it's a lathe job, welding repair, vehicle reconditioning, or custom fabrication, we approach every project with the same dedication to quality.

What We Do

Our Services

We offer a comprehensive range of mechanical and engineering services to meet all your workshop needs.

🚙

Vehicle Reconditioning

Complete mechanical reconditioning of jeeps, cars, and heavy vehicles. Engine overhaul, component repair and restoration to bring your vehicle back to peak performance.

  • Jeep & Car Reconditioning
  • Heavy Vehicle Overhaul
  • Engine & Transmission Repair
  • Component Restoration
⚙️

Lathe Machining Works

Precision lathe machining for shafts, bushings, pulleys, and custom metal components. Tight tolerances maintained for industrial and agricultural applications.

  • Shaft Turning & Grinding
  • Bushing & Bearing Seats
  • Pulley Machining
  • Custom Component Fabrication
🔥

Welding Works

Expert welding services for mild steel, stainless steel, and structural work. From simple repairs to complex structural fabrication, we deliver strong, clean welds.

  • Arc & MIG Welding
  • Structural Steel Welding
  • Vehicle Frame Repair
  • Pipe & Fitting Welding
🔧

Custom Fabrication & Repair

Custom metal fabrication and general mechanical repair for agricultural equipment, industrial machinery, and commercial vehicles. No job is too big or too small.

  • Agricultural Equipment Repair
  • Custom Metal Fabrication
  • Industrial Machinery Repair
  • Hydraulic Component Service
Our Equipment

Workshop Machines

We invest in quality equipment to ensure precision results for every job.

🔩

Lathe Machine

Heavy-duty lathe machine for turning, boring, and threading precision metal components. Handles a wide range of diameters and materials.

⚡

Welding Machine

Industrial welding machine capable of arc welding and MIG/MAG welding. Delivers consistent, high-strength welds on mild steel and structural sections.

🔄

Drilling Machine

Pillar drilling machine for precise hole-making in metal components. Accommodates various drill bit sizes for different job requirements.

🌀

Grinding Machine

Surface and cylindrical grinding machine for finishing and sharpening metal parts to exact specifications and smooth surface finishes.

💪

Hydraulic Press

High-tonnage hydraulic press for pressing, straightening, and assembling heavy components. Ideal for bearing installation and shaft alignment work.
